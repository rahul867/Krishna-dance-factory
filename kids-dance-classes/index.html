Frequently Asked Questions

What parents usually ask us

What are the fees for kids dance classes?

Fees depend on the batch and duration. Call or WhatsApp us on 095993 53649 and we'll share the current fee structure — and remember, your child's first class is free.

Does my child need any dance experience?

Not at all. Most children join as complete beginners. Classes are built step-by-step so every child keeps up comfortably.

When are the classes held?

Kids batches run on Saturdays and Sundays, with a free trial class every Saturday. Call us to find the batch that suits your child's age.

Can parents watch the class?

Yes — you're welcome to watch your child's trial class and see how they settle in.

What should my child wear?

Comfortable clothes they can move in — a t-shirt, track pants or shorts, and sports shoes. Bring a water bottle too.
