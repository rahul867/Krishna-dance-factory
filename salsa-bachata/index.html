Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your first class

Can complete beginners join?

Yes. The weekend batches are beginner-friendly and start with the fundamentals, so you do not need any previous dance experience.

Do I need to bring a partner?

No. You can come solo. Partners rotate during class so everyone gets a chance to learn and practise.

When are the Salsa and Bachata classes held?

Classes run in weekend batches. Call or WhatsApp us for the latest Saturday and Sunday time slots.

Is the first trial class free?

Yes, subject to availability. Free-trial spots are limited in each weekend batch, so please confirm your slot on WhatsApp before visiting.

What are the class fees?

Fees depend on the batch and programme. WhatsApp us and we will share the current fee structure along with available timings.

What should I wear?

Wear comfortable clothes that allow easy movement and clean, comfortable footwear suitable for a dance floor.
